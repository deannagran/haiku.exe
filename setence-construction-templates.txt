standard sentence constructions:

* noun > verb > definite article > noun
she	 ate	    the		   cake

* adverb [COMMA] > definite article > noun > verb
lazily,			the	      fox    walked

* noun > verb > adverb
 he	ran	quickly

*

more poetic constructions:

* verb [COMMA] > verb [COMMA] > verb 
drinking, 	dancing,	laughing

* adverb > verb 
peacefully sleeping
